Scholarships & Waivers

Scholarships for Children

At Glacier Trek, we strongly believe that children must trek. Children aged 14 and below qualify for a 30% trek fee scholarship. Check out this article.

Waivers

For every ten adult trekkers (aged 15 and above) in a group booking, we will waive off the trek fee for one trekker. Glacier Trek will reimburse the said trek fee after you complete your trek.

(These benefits will be applicable only if our team is informed at experience@glaciertrek.com after completion of your trek and within 30 days from your trek date. Requests made beyond this period will not be eligible.)
