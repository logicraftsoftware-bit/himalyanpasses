Understanding the Role of Pole Line Fitting in Power Infrastructure

A pole line fitting is more than just a piece of galvanized steel; it is a precision-engineered interface between the support structure and the live conductor. Whether it is a distribution line in a bustling city or a high-tension transmission line traversing mountains, the hardware must withstand mechanical tension, wind loads, and environmental corrosion.
